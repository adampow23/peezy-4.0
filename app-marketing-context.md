## Value Proposition

- **Problem:** Moving hides dozens of time-sensitive tasks, vendor decisions, address updates, and admin chores that people often discover too late.
- **Target Audience:** U.S. adults planning a residential move, especially busy renters, homeowners, families, and long-distance movers who want less mental load.
- **Unique Differentiator:** Peezy turns a short moving assessment into a personalized moving plan, then guides the user through one calm daily action while offering vendor coordination, inventory scanning, and support chat.
- **Elevator Pitch:** Peezy is a personal moving concierge that builds your custom move plan and helps you handle the stressful parts one task at a time.
