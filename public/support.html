Common Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. To cancel: open the Settings app on your iPhone → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions → Peezy+ → Cancel Subscription. You'll keep access until the end of your current billing period.

How do I delete my account?

Open Peezy → Settings tab → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account, tasks, and all associated data within 30 days. If you have an active subscription, cancel it through Apple's subscription management before deleting your account.

How do I restore my purchases?

Open Peezy → Settings tab → Restore Purchases. Or, on the paywall screen, tap "Restore Purchases" below the subscription buttons. You must be signed in with the Apple ID that originally made the purchase.

The inventory scanner isn't working — what should I do?

Make sure you've granted Peezy permission to use your camera (Settings app → Peezy → Camera → enabled). If the camera permission was denied, you'll need to re-enable it in iOS Settings. If you continue having trouble, email us at adampow23@gmail.com with a description of what you're seeing.
