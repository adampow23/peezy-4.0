Privacy Policy

Peezy — AI Moving Concierge

Effective Date: March 28, 2026 | Last Updated: March 28, 2026

Peezy ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Peezy mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you use the App.

By using Peezy, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

Account Information: Your name, email address, and authentication credentials. If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive the name and email associated with that account.

Moving Assessment Data: When you complete the Peezy assessment, you provide details about your move, including your current and new addresses, move date, dwelling types, whether you rent or own, household composition, moving distance and preferences, and service preferences.

Financial and Service Preferences: Categories of financial institutions, healthcare providers, fitness memberships, and insurance policies you use. We collect category types such as "bank account" or "dentist," not account numbers or login credentials.

Chat Messages: When you chat with Peezy's AI concierge, we collect the messages you send and the responses generated.

Workflow Responses: When you complete vendor qualification surveys within the App, we collect your responses.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and App version.

Usage Data: How you interact with the App, including which tasks you complete, snooze, or skip, features you use, and session duration.

Location Data: We use your current and new addresses (which you provide) to calculate moving distance, determine interstate moves, and identify available service providers in your area. We use Apple's MapKit and Core Location services to geocode addresses you enter. We do not track your real-time GPS location in the background.

Crash and Performance Data: If the App crashes, Firebase Crashlytics collects crash logs, device state, and stack traces to help us diagnose and fix issues.

1.3 Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect Social Security numbers, bank account numbers, credit card numbers, login credentials for your financial or healthcare accounts, health records or medical data, or biometric data. We do not access your contacts, photos, microphone, or camera for the core moving concierge features.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide and operate the App, including generating your personalized moving task list, powering the AI chat concierge, connecting you with vetted service providers, sending you task reminders, and managing your account and subscription. We also use your information to improve the App by analyzing usage patterns, monitoring stability through crash reporting, and understanding which features are most helpful. Additionally, we use your information to communicate with you by responding to support requests, sending service-related notifications, and notifying you about important changes.

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 Third-Party AI Service — Anthropic (Claude)

When you use Peezy's AI chat feature, your messages and relevant moving context are sent to Anthropic's Claude API for processing.

Specifically, the data sent to Anthropic includes your chat messages and conversation history, your name and general moving context (move date, distance, dwelling type), and the current task you are asking about. We do not send Anthropic your full address, financial account details, or any information beyond what is needed to generate a helpful response.

Anthropic's data handling: Peezy uses Anthropic's commercial API, which means your data is not used to train Anthropic's AI models. API data is retained by Anthropic for up to 30 days for trust and safety purposes, then automatically deleted. Anthropic does not sell your data to third parties. For more information, see Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

Your control: Before any data is sent to Anthropic's AI service, the App will ask for your explicit consent. You can decline, in which case the AI chat feature will not be available, but all other App features will continue to work. You can change this preference at any time in the App's Settings.

3.2 Firebase (Google)

We use Google's Firebase platform for authentication, data storage (Firestore), server-side processing (Cloud Functions), and crash reporting (Crashlytics). Google processes this data as a data processor on our behalf. For details, see Firebase Privacy and Security.

3.3 Service Providers (Vendor Coordination)

When you complete a vendor workflow, we share relevant preferences with our support team to coordinate services on your behalf. We share only the information necessary to match you with appropriate providers. We do not share your information with vendors until you explicitly request a service through the App.

3.4 Payment Processing

Subscription payments are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect, process, or store your payment card information.

3.5 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, in response to valid legal process, to protect the rights or safety of Peezy, our users, or others, or to enforce our Terms of Service.

3.6 No Sale of Personal Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing, advertising, or any other purpose.

4. Data Retention

Account and assessment data is retained for as long as your account is active. Chat messages are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored on our servers beyond the active session; conversation context sent to Anthropic is retained by Anthropic for up to 30 days. Crash reports are retained by Firebase Crashlytics for 90 days. Task and workflow data is retained for the duration of your active account.

When you request account deletion, we delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including encryption of data in transit using TLS/SSL, secure storage in Firebase with access controls, server-side processing through Cloud Functions, and no storage of payment information on our servers.

While we take reasonable steps to protect your data, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Account Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated personal data directly within the App by going to Settings and selecting "Delete Account." This will permanently remove your account information, assessment data, tasks, workflow responses, and chat history. Account deletion is processed within 30 days. If you have an active subscription, you will need to cancel it through Apple's subscription management.

6.2 AI Chat Consent

You can enable or disable AI chat data sharing at any time in the App's Settings.

6.3 Access and Correction

You can view and update your assessment data, move details, and preferences within the App at any time.

7. State Privacy Rights

7.1 California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, use, and disclose; to request deletion of your personal information; to opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data); and to non-discrimination for exercising your rights. Contact us at privacy@peezy.move.

7.2 Other U.S. State Privacy Laws

Residents of Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah, Texas, Oregon, Montana, and other states with comprehensive privacy laws have similar rights. Contact us at privacy@peezy.move.

8. Children's Privacy

Peezy is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child, we will take steps to delete it promptly. Contact us at privacy@peezy.move.

9. International Users

Peezy is currently designed for users in the United States. If you access the App from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated Policy within the App and updating the "Last Updated" date.
